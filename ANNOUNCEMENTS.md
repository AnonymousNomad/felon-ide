### FELON IDE: Open-Source Voice Assistant That Respects Your Privacy

We just shipped FELON IDE v4.0 — a universal model platform that lets you load, train, chain, compile, and deploy AI models entirely on-device.

**The vision:** An open-source alternative to Alexa/Google/Siri that runs completely locally. Your voice commands never touch a cloud server. No telemetry. No subscription. No data mining.

**Current capabilities:**
- Load any model (file upload, HuggingFace, P2P mesh)
- Train, fine-tune, distill, chain teacher→student
- Android APK builder (generate → build → verify → install → run)
- Decentralized P2P repo network (init, publish, clone, push, pull)
- Nanobot swarm tool system (10 tool types, 1,000 nanobot slots)
- White Rabbit truth-seeking engine (consent-gated)

**Training progress:**
Our 18.4M parameter FsiDeepCore transformer is at step 17,500/200,000:
- 99.32% accuracy, 0.014 loss
- Crossed 99% threshold in the last 6 hours
- Self-healing immortal trainer, zero crashes
- Q-NFRE knowledge engine with 67k entries
- Chimera neural routing across 6 organs (phi=0.80)

**What's next:**
- Complete training to convergence
